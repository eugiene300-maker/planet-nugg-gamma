About Planet Nugg — Long Island's Premier Cannabis Destination

Planet Nugg redefines cannabis retail on Long Island. Opened in September 2024 at 2043 Wellwood Ave, Farmingdale, our 5,000-square-foot space offers over 1,700 premium, lab-tested products—the largest selection in town. Founded by wellness entrepreneur David Tubens, we blend futuristic design with unparalleled customer service.
